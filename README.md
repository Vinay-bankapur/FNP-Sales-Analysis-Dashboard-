##  Business Problem
Ferns N Petals (FNP) wants to understand customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, and product performance to improve sales strategy and optimize delivery efficiency.

##  Objectives
- Analyze revenue trends across occasions and categories  
- Identify top-performing products  
- Understand customer ordering patterns  
- Evaluate delivery performance

##  Tools & Techniques
- Microsoft Excel (Advanced)
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- KPI Design  
- Interactive Dashboard (Slicers)

##  Data Analysis Process
1. Data Cleaning (removed duplicates, handled missing values)
2. Data Transformation (created delivery time metric)
3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
4. KPI Identification
5. Dashboard Design & Visualization

##  Key Insights
- Occasion-based sales show that Anniversary and Birthday contribute the highest revenue, indicating strong emotional buying behavior.
- Soft Toys dominate product category sales, suggesting high demand for giftable items.
- Peak ordering time occurs during midday, highlighting customer activity trends.
- Delivery time averages around 4 days, which may impact customer satisfaction.

##  Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing occasions (Anniversary, Birthday)
- Increase inventory for top-selling categories like Soft Toys
- Run promotions during peak hours (12 PM – 2 PM)
- Improve delivery logistics to reduce turnaround time

- Add predictive analysis for sales forecasting
